What's QuirkyWeb?

Welcome to QuirkyWeb, a unique collection of random and innovative websites developed by the creative minds of Humber College's 'Web Development' students. Created by Ozan Sereflioglu and Joshua Moshood, QuirkyWeb showcases the diversity, creativity, and technical prowess of our student community. Each project reflects a blend of quirky ideas and cutting-edge web technologies, offering visitors a delightful and surprising digital experience.

Our mission is to provide a platform for budding developers to experiment, innovate, and share their work with a broader audience. At QuirkyWeb, you'll find a variety of web applications that range from the whimsical to the practical, all designed to entertain, inform, and inspire.
